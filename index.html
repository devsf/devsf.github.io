Studies & Working

I have been studying Computer Science since I was 14. I studied at "I.T.I. N. Copernico - Carpeggiani" high school of Ferrara and then got a degree from the University of Ferrara in 2010.
Being first a Java developer helped me write my thesis (ATT: Automatic Testing Tool) during the MEEO internship and find my first job as a Java Web Developer at CSE (Consorzio Servizi Bancari) S.c.a.r.l. (started on February, 1st 2011).
During my 4 years and a half at CSE I deepened my Java knowledge using Eclipse RCP/SWT, JEE, Spring Framework, Google Web Toolkit (GWT), XML, XSD, JAXB and IBM DB2 technologies.
In November 2015 I started working at MEEO as a Web Developer. Currently I am developing using Python & Django, Dockers & Kubernetes, Bootstrap 4, HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript & jQuery, bash scripting, PostgreSQL databases in a GNU/Linux environment.

Music & Drums

Music is my second passion. I have always listened to Music in every moment of my life.
In 2008, I started playing and teaching acoustic guitar to children, and in 2012 I started practising my favourite instrument: the drums.
Currently studying at Hybryd Music School, Making Music School has been my music school from 2013 to 2018 (Gorilla Music School then named Making Music School in 2014) but I started practising at AMF (Associazione Musicisti di Ferrara) in 2012.

Outburst & NEBULΔ

In October 2015, I joined an Italian Pop-Rock group located in Ferrara named Outburst as a drummer and sometimes as a song-writer. In February 2018, we started working on our first EP.
Working on the EP lead us to change sounds and to start a new project named NEBULΔ, in June 2018. The EP, "Buio Accecante", was released in March 2019 preceded by the first single "Orizzonte".

Books, Chocolate & more

IT and Music are not the only passions! I really love reading (specially Tolkien's books) and eating chocolate (...sometimes while reading...)! I do like fantasy and crime novels (sometimes in English), but the book category is not so important. What matters is reading something!

Future
